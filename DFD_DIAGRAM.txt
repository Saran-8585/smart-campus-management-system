                            DATA STORES INDEX
================================================================================

    D1 ── USERS                (id, name, email, password, role, ...)
    D2 ── SUBJECTS             (id, name, code, department, faculty_id, ...)
    D3 ── TIMETABLE            (id, subject_id, day, time, room, is_active, ...)
    D4 ── ATTENDANCE           (id, student_id, subject_id, date, status)
    D5 ── MARKS                (id, student_id, subject_id, exam_type, score, ...)

    D7 ── ENROLLMENTS          (id, student_id, subject_id)
    D8 ── NAVIGATION_PLACES    (id, name, block, floor, directions, map_x/y, ...)
    D9 ── LOST_FOUND_ITEMS     (id, posted_by, type, item_name, status, ...)
    D10 ─ LOST_FOUND_CLAIMS    (id, item_id, claimant_id, status, ...)
